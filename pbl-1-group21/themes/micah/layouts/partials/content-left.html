A Historical Example of Fecal Contamination

One prominent historical example of fecal matter contamination was in the first English settlement of North America, Jamestown. Waste from the colonists' outhouses seeped into James river, which allowed the waste to penetrate the aquifer that they colonists used as their water source Cohen History water supply(Cohen History, 2020). Much of the disease in death in the Jamestown colony could be partially attributed to this grossly dangerous contaminant which could have been a major factor in the failure of the colony.
